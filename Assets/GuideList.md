# Battlecruisers Reference Guide

## Cruisers

**AlphaSpace** | Sentient, amphibious colossus; albeit stuck in a semi-transformed position. Rocket construction comes natural to him, aircraft construction does not. 
**Arkedeso** | Which way is it facing? What happened? These questions and more answered* with 3 drones and double build speed for buildings and ships. *Never addressed again.

**Axiom** | Copyright-infringement-evasion is the main perk of this luxury interplanetary meat-people cruise-liner. Has a unique internal shield slot secreted inside its soft belly.

**BasicRig** | A simple rig with a minimum number of slots. Starts with six drones and has the most powerful shield boost of all.

**BlackRig** | Sinister cruiser in disguise: means of eluding UAC patrol, and a trap for merchants seeking the refuge of a deep sea rig. Its turret capabilities are stuff of legend.

**Bullshark** | Tough cruiser that boosts the build speed and fire rate of all defensives.

**Cricket** | All buildings are built multiple times sturdier at no extra cost. Think of the free hit points!

**Eagle** | Light cruiser with cheaper Production structures.

**Endless Wall** | A fortified wall with sturdy buildings but weaker shields. A weak spot would be nice, but all the volatile stuff is safely tucked away under that crane.

**Essex** | A serious battlecruiser for sensible robots. 7 hard-working British drones who take their time building structures, but by the devs do we produce aircraft quickly!

**Flea** | Agile water-striding cruiser hull. Facilitates high-quality ship and aircraft factories, which build their units with greater efficiency.

**Fort Nova** | Fortress built around an experimental warhead, rather than moving the warhead to a secure location.

**Fortress Prime** | The first fortress, built to withstand the most ferocious battlecruiser onslaughts.

**Goatherd** | Large, versatile battlecruiser with cheaper mast structures.

**Hammerhead** | Sturdy cruiser with faster building SeaFactories and ships.

**Huntress Prime** | Keeps following us, seems to be warning us about something. Oh well!

**Longbow** | Massive cruiser with faster building AirFactories and aircraft.

**Man O' War** | Scary scary boss thing

**Megalith** | A famous prince piloting the Megalith famously proved himself a better fighter than dozens of meat-people civilian tower blocks. All its buildings have increased health.

**Megalodon** | Massive cruiser with faster building ultraweapons.

**Microlodon** | Little brother of the mighty megalodon-class, with the fastest-building ultraweapons on Flooded Earth.

**Middlodon** | Precisely half way between Megalodon and Microlodon. Not quite as good as either, as the middle child always knows.

**October** | Stealth nuclear submarine, starts in a cloud of stealth and specializes in aircraft production.

**Orac** | Lower health buildings in exchange for much faster shield regeneration.

**Pistol** | Deceptively powerful cruiser hull with faster-firing, more powerful rockets and missile weapons.

**Raptor** | Light cruiser with faster shield build and recharge rates.

**Rickshaw** | Five platform slots supported by a sturdy, reliable, Rust Industries chinesium "metal" alloy hull. The manufacturer kindly offers remote assistance in building intelligence and tactical structures.

**Rockjaw** | Sturdy cruiser with faster build speeds and fire rates for all offensives.

**Salvage** | Ramshackle cruiser pieces bolted to an impossibly-heavy cylinder discovered by the owner. It vibrates and hums and is heavier than cruisers 10x its size.

**Shepherd** | King-size cruiser with better fire rates on defensive and offensive weapons. Named after wool farmers, for some reason.

**TasDevil** | Broken demon from down under with shield boost. Built like a brick waste-disposal shelter.

**TekGnosis** | Mountain-sized UAC juggernaut. Once built, any structure shall not be destroyed. May devs have mercy on your core.

**Trident** | General purpose cruiser with faster build rates for all tactical buildings.

**Yeti** | Enormous Antarctic ice-breaker, re-purposed as a fully-functional battlecruiser. Houses the Compensator-107 drone overclocker, for a cheaper workforce.

**Yucalux** | Outlandish Rust Industries battlecruiser designed to boasting the fastest, most hastily-assembled aircraft in town!

**Zumwalt** | Technically a stealth destroyer, with a pop-up stealth generator and better offensive rocket technology.

## Buildings

**2Z DroneStation** | Provides 2 extra BuilderDrones, improving your economy. Unlocks more advanced buildables.

**4Z DroneStation** | Comes complete with 4 BuilderDrones.

**6Z DroneStation** | Comes complete with 6 BuilderDrones.

**8Z DroneStation** | Comes complete with 8 BuilderDrones.

**AirFactory** | Produces aircraft.

**AirTurret** | Basic anti-air defence that protects you against aircraft.

**Artillery** | Powerful but inaccurate anti-cruiser weapon. Countered by shields.

**BlastVLS** | Structure-seeking missile launcher. Countered by Tesla.

**Booster** | Improves the performance of adjacent buildings. Increases turret fire rate, shield recharge rate and factory production rate.

**Broadside Cannon** | Devastating anti-cruiser cannon battery that easily blast through shields.

**Cannon** | Light long-range weapon for early attacks on the enemy cruiser and its buildings

**CIWS** | Close In Weapon System, a versatile ship, missile and aircraft defence turret. Occupies a utility slot.

**CoastGuard** | Powerful long range ship defence. Platform-mounted triple-barrel rotary cannons that grant domination of the sea lane.

**ControlTower** | Increases the movement speed of all your aircraft. This bonus stacks.

**Deathstar Launcher** | Designed to surgically strike high-value enemy structures from the safety of orbit. Inhibited by shields. Satellite can't be remote-boosted.

**DroneFactory** | Produces Drones over time.

**FirecrackerVLS** | Vertical Launch System for firecracker missiles. Targets groups of enemy buildings with low accuracy. Countered by Tesla, CIWS or Shields.

**FlakTurret** | Powerful anti-air defence that shreds aircraft. Area of effect damage increases effect on clusters of aircraft.

**GatlingMortar** | Inaccurate long-range offensive mortar that rains shells upon the enemy cruiser. Countered by shields.

**GlobeShield** | Powerful shield with enormous all-around coverage. Turtle power!

**GrapheneBarrier** | Provides physical protection from low-angle attacks.

**IonCannon** | Accelerates ionized aluminium molecules straight across the sea, melting everything in its path.

**Kamikaze Signal** | Sends a one time kamikaze signal to all aircraft, making them crash into the enemy cruiser. Great for turning a dominant air force into flying bombs.

**LasCannon** | Highly accurate anti-cruiser laser. Countered by many shields.

**LocalBooster** | Improves the performance of adjacent buildings. Increases turret fire rate, shield recharge rate and factory production rate.

**MissilePod** | Versatile and accurate missile launcher. Targets anything except other missiles.

**MissileRevolver** | Fires a triple-salvo of powerful, pinpoint-accurate rockets at the enemy cruiser. Countered by tesla coils.

**MLRS** | Multiple-launch rocket system, fires a ripple salvo of loosely-guided missiles at the enemy cruiser.

**Mortar** | Powerful, long-range but inaccurate anti-ship defence.

**NovaArtillery** | Pinpoint accurate artillery. Delivers high-explosive anti-armor warheads with precision.

**Nuke Launcher** | Launches a nuclear warhead that destroys the enemy cruiser. There are no counters that we know of...

**RailCannon** | High-damage, slow-firing, precision cannon. Super-conducting rails accelerate a cryo-hardened tungsten dart to Mach 16.

**RocketLauncher** | Fires a triple-salvo of powerful, pinpoint-accurate rockets at the enemy cruiser. Countered by tesla coils.

**SAMSite** | Excellent anti-air defence with long range.

**SeaFactory** | Produces ships.

**SectorShield** | Mast-based protection from high-angle attacks.

**Shield** | Creates a regenerating shield that absorbs incoming fire.

**ShipTurret** | Basic, short-range anti-ship defence soley designed to destroy light attacking ships.

**Sledgehammer** | The only gun that fires 5 shells in 5 milliseconds. Supported by a dual firecracker missile system.

**SpySatellite Launcher** | Launches a SpySatellite which counters enemy stealth.

**StealthGenerator** | Hides your cruiser from the enemy so they cannot see what buildings you are constructing. Countered by SpySatellites.

**TeslaCoil** | Destroys incoming rockets at long range, but slow to recharge.

**UltraCIWS** | Ultramatic Close-In Weapon System. A powerful turret that shoots down enemy missiles, aircraft and ships.

**Ultralisk** | Provides 30 BuilderDrones, making for an incredible economy.

## Units

**Archon** | Terrifyingly powerful "ship", best countered by many Gunships.

**AttackBoat** | Fast but weak early assault craft, armed with a short-range rocket mortar.

**AttackRIB** | Fast attack ship with long range anti-structure capability.

**Bomber** | Slow aircraft that bombs the enemy cruiser. Countered by anti-air.

**Broadsword** | Overpowered offensive gunship. Armed with anti-surface rockets and a CIWS minigun omni-turret.

**Deathstar** | A satellite that destroys everything in its path. Can only be destroyed by destroying its uplink structure.

**DefenceRaft** | Floats out to midway, providing early aircraft, ship and missile interception.

**Destroyer** | Powerful ship armed with long-range missile arrays. Countered by gunships and mortars.

**Fighter** | Fast aircraft that patrols your cruiser protecting you from enemy aircraft.

**FireBoat** | Volatile, floating flamethrower pushed along by a single motor. Dangerous for all involved.

**FlakTurtle** | Shield support ship armed with anti-air cannons.

**Frigate** | Strong ship with excellent air defence and a medium-range blast mortar. Countered by mortars.

**GlassCannoneer** | Extremely weak black glass boat armed with an over-powered direct-fire cannon.

**Guardian** | Powerful defense aircraft, able to shoot down aircraft and sink incoming ships. Patrols your territory.

**GunBoat** | Tanky frigate with three mid-range ballistic cannons. Countered by shields and mortars.

**Gunship** | Heavy aircraft that patrols your cruiser protecting you from enemy ships.

**Huntress Unit** | Just a regular trident cruiser. Nothing to see here.

**Kamikazee** | Warhead with wings. Crashes into the enemy cruiser and detonates its significant onboard payload.

**Kamikazoo** | Massive blast warhead with wings. 911s into the enemy cruiser, machine guns blazing.

**ManOfWarBoss** | Scary scary boss thing

**MissileFighter** | Fast interceptor that targets aircraft and naval units. Armed with high-explosive, highly unstable missiles.

**NukeBomber** | Bomber with insta-death payload. Shoot it down!

**RocketTurtle** | Shield support ship armed with long-range missiles.

**SiegeDestroyer** | Powerful ship armed with a long-range rotary ballistic mortar. Countered by gunships.

**SpyPlane** | Counters stealth, as long as it is alive. Patrols your territory.

**SpySatellite** | Counters enemy stealth, allowing you to see the enemy cruiser.

**SteamCopter** | Light attack gunship, fires pneumatic slugs at enemy surface units and buildings.

**StratBomber** | High-altitude precision bomber, not that precision matters with such an excessive warhead.
